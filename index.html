Products

What we build

First Product

Fascinator

Fidget Lab  ·  iOS App

A physics-based 3D fidget toy for iOS — built entirely in Swift and Metal, without traditional 3D asset files or game engines. Every object is procedurally generated: geometry, normals, lighting, and physics computed from first principles.

  • Real-time 3D rendering via Apple Metal
  • 15 interactive objects across curated collections
  • Physics simulation: angular momentum, damping, quaternion rotation
  • Localized in 14 languages
  • iOS 17.0 · iPhone & iPad
Available on the App Store

About

The engineer behind the algorithms

Cheyenne Algorithms LLC is a software engineering company based in Colorado Springs, Colorado — in the shadow of Cheyenne Mountain. Founded by a retired professional software engineer with decades of experience across technical domains.

The company was built on a precise thesis: AI is an amplifier, not a replacement. An experienced engineer using AI tools can deliver what previously required a team — with the same rigor, the same correctness requirements, and a fraction of the overhead.

Fascinator: Fidget Lab is the proof. A complete iOS application — real-time 3D physics, Metal GPU rendering, 14-language localization, App Store distribution — built by one engineer, leveraging AI as a coding partner. Every architectural decision was made by a human who knew exactly what to ask, and why.

Consulting engagements open to defense, aerospace, and business software clients where precision and accountability matter.
