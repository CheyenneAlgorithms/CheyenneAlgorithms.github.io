Privacy Policy

Effective: April 2026  ·  Applies to: Fascinate (iOS)

The Short Version

Fascinate does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not know who you are, where you are, or how you use the app.

Data We Collect

None. Fascinate has no user accounts, no registration, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind. The app runs entirely on your device.

In-App Purchases

Fascinate offers optional shape purchases through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple. Cheyenne Algorithms never receives, sees, or stores your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all purchase transactions and can be found at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Third-Party Services

Fascinate does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data collection services.

Children

Fascinate does not collect information from anyone, including children. The app is appropriate for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, the effective date above will be updated. We will never introduce data collection without updating this policy first.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@cheyennealgorithms.com.
