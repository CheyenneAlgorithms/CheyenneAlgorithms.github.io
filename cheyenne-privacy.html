Privacy Policy

Effective: April 2026  ·  Applies to: cheyennealgorithms.com

The Short Version

This policy covers the Cheyenne Algorithms LLC website — cheyennealgorithms.com and its pages, including this blog. It does not cover our iOS app, Fascinator, which has its own privacy policy.

Data We Collect

This website does not currently use analytics, advertising, cookies, or any third-party tracking services. We do not operate a contact form, newsletter signup, or user account system at this time. Standard web server logs maintained by GitHub Pages, our hosting provider, may include basic technical information such as IP address and browser type — this data is governed by GitHub's own privacy practices and is not accessed or used by Cheyenne Algorithms LLC for any purpose.

Email Contact

If you email us directly — for support, consulting inquiries, or any other reason — we will have access to your email address and the contents of your message. We use this information solely to respond to you and do not share it with third parties.

Changes to This Policy

If we introduce new data collection on this website — such as a contact form, analytics, or mailing list — this policy will be updated first, and the effective date above will reflect the change.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@cheyennealgorithms.com.
